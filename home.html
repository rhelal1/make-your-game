Graph Go back to your work where you left it last time! GO TO PISCINE PacMan

In this newly introduced academic program, known as 'Piscine Pacman' students are required to successfully complete various projects in order to complete it. Each project consists of a set number of audits that students must pass. However, it should be noted that if a student fails three audits within a project, they will be deemed unsuccessful and will be required to repeat the project. As part of the evaluation process, there are three auditors who have the authority to assign a failing grade: Amail, Fatabbas, Emahfood, and Akhaled
